\## Summary




As a customer, I want visibility into active and previous orders so I can trust the delivery flow and revisit past purchases.




\## Acceptance Criteria




\- Customer can view active order progress with status updates.

\- Customer can see key tracking metadata (order id, ETA, current state).

\- Customer can view a list of past orders.

\- Past orders show at least basic order summary details.
