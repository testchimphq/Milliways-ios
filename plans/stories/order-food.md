\## Summary




As a customer, I want to browse the Milliways menu, add items to a cart, and place an order so I can receive food without friction.




\## Acceptance Criteria




\- Customer can browse menu categories and items.

\- Customer can add one or more items to the cart.

\- Customer can adjust item quantity before checkout.

\- Customer can complete checkout with valid delivery details.
