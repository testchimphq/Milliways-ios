## Prerequisites

- App is launched and customer is on the menu screen.

## Test Steps

1. Select a menu item and add it to cart.
2. Open the cart view.
3. Increase quantity by one.
4. Decrease quantity back to original.

## Expected Behaviour

- Selected item is added to cart.
- Quantity changes are reflected correctly in cart UI.
- Cart total updates as quantity changes.
