## Prerequisites

- Customer has at least one completed order.

## Test Steps

1. Navigate to order history.
2. Open a previous order entry.
3. Review the order summary details.

## Expected Behaviour

- Completed orders are listed in reverse chronological order.
- Selecting an entry opens order details.
- Order details show item and pricing summary.
